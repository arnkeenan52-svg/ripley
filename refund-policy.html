Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

At Ripley, we aim to provide reliable, high-quality AI-powered support tools. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be granted for subscriptions or services.

1. General Policy

All payments made for Ripley subscriptions, premium plans, or related services are considered final and non-refundable unless otherwise stated.

By purchasing or subscribing, you acknowledge that you have reviewed the features, pricing, and limitations associated with your selected plan.

2. Exceptional Circumstances

Refunds may be considered only under limited and exceptional conditions, including:

- Duplicate or accidental charges
- Critical service failures preventing basic functionality

In such cases, requests must be submitted within 7 days of the transaction, along with sufficient details for evaluation.

3. No Refunds for Misuse

Refunds will not be issued for issues resulting from:

- Incorrect configuration or setup
- Misuse or abuse of the service
- Violations of our Terms of Service
- Lack of understanding of features

Users are responsible for ensuring proper use and compatibility before purchase.

4. Subscriptions and Cancellations

Subscriptions may be cancelled at any time through your billing provider or dashboard.

However, cancellation does not entitle you to a refund for the current billing period. Your access will remain active until the end of the billing cycle.

5. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund under the conditions above, you may submit a request by:

- Contacting us through our official Discord support server
- Sending an email to support@yourdomain.com

Please include:

- Payment details or transaction ID
- Description of the issue
- Any relevant screenshots or evidence

6. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Continued use of Ripley services after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.
