Refund Policy

1. General Policy
All payments made for ArcBot premium plans or services are considered final and non-refundable. By subscribing, you agree to the features, limitations, and duration described at the time of purchase.

2. Exceptional Circumstances
Refunds may be considered only in rare cases, such as double charges or a critical service malfunction that prevents basic usage. In these cases, please contact support within 7 days of the transaction to request an evaluation.

3. No Refunds For Misuse
Refunds will not be issued for issues caused by user error, improper setup, abuse of the bot, or violation of our Terms of Use. It is your responsibility to ensure compatibility and proper usage.

4. Subscriptions and Cancellations
You may cancel your subscription at any time, but cancellations do not trigger a refund for the remaining period. The service will continue until the end of the current billing cycle.

5. How to Request a Refund
To request a refund under the exceptions above, contact us through our Discord support server or via support@yourdomain.com with your payment details and reason for the request.
