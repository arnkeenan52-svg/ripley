Privacy Policy

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how ArcBot collects, uses, and protects your data when you interact with our bot on Discord. By using ArcBot, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Data We Collect
ArcBot collects minimal data required to function. This may include user IDs, server IDs, message IDs, usernames, and configuration settings. No personal or sensitive data (like emails, passwords, or payment details) is ever collected.

3. How We Use Data
The data collected is used solely to provide and enhance bot features such as moderation, custom commands, server configuration, and related functionalities. We do not sell, share, or distribute any collected data to third parties.

4. Data Storage
All stored data is kept securely in our databases and is only accessible to authorized team members. Configuration data and logs are retained only as long as needed for bot functionality or until removed by server administrators.

5. Third-Party Services
ArcBot may use third-party APIs (such as Discord’s API) to operate effectively. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services but only interact with them in accordance with Discord’s guidelines.

6. User Control and Data Removal
Server owners can remove ArcBot at any time, which automatically ends data collection for that server. If you wish to request the deletion of stored data, please contact us via our support Discord or email.

7. Protection of Information
We implement appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure of your data. While no system is entirely immune to threats, we take your privacy seriously and act swiftly in case of any concerns.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or changes in how ArcBot operates. We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed about how your data is handled.

9. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, feel free to reach out to us via our support Discord or email us at support@yourdomain.com.
